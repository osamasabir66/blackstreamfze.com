About Our Company

About Us & Our Skills

Black Stream FZE* is a leading company specializing in the trading of refined petroleum and energy products. With over 35 years of experience in the petroleum and petrochemical sectors, we have forged strong partnerships with top-tier refineries, allowing us to source and supply high-quality products tailored to our customers' specific needs.

Our success and reputation in the market are grounded in our integrity, exceptional performance, and an impeccable track record in energy trading. We have a deep understanding of local cultures, which enables us to cultivate meaningful cross-cultural relationships with producers and customers alike. This unique advantage enhances our service and strengthens our position in the industry.

We are committed to executing every opportunity through strategic planning and optimal supply chain management. Our primary goal is to meet customer needs with quality products, including:

  1. - Bitumen
  2. - Bitumen Membranes
  3. - Base Oils (SN 50, SN 70, SN 150, SN 300, SN 500)
  4. - Fuel Oils
  5. - Rubber Processing Oil
  6. - Low Aromatic White Spirit
  7. - D2 Diesel Oil
  8. - Sulphur Lumps
  9. - Methanol
  10. - Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG)
  11. - Crude Oil
  12. - Urea

In recent years, the global energy markets have faced numerous challenges, yet our management team has systematically built on its expertise, achieving total sales exceeding $5 million across 100 cargo shipments worldwide. Our proven performance, combined with a clear understanding of local cultures, gives us a distinct competitive edge.

Quality Assurance

At Black Stream FZE, we ensure that our products comply with both local and international standards, including ASTM and BSEN. Each product undergoes rigorous testing by third-party inspectors from reputable independent laboratories at the time of shipment, guaranteeing the highest quality and accurate quantity.

Our Commitment

Located in Phase I of Hamriyah Free Zone, Sharjah, UAE, Black Stream FZE believes in the power of teamwork. We understand that together, everyone achieves more. By uniting diverse skills and experiences, we turn our collective strengths into unparalleled success.

We prioritize honesty and integrity in all our relationships, listening first and acting transparently. Our commitment to responsibility drives us to set ambitious targets and make informed decisions to achieve them, rewarding those who excel in their efforts.

Effective risk management is crucial to our operations. Our ability to analyze, identify, and mitigate risks ensures timely deliveries for our clients. We also recognize our responsibility to preserve and protect the environment for future generations.
